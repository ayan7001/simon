How to play?

  • Click the “Start Game” button to begin.
  • Simon will light up a random button. Memorize the lit button.
  • Click on the button that lit up earlier. If correct, you’ll advance to the next level.
  • A new button will light up. Memorize all the buttons in the sequence that were previously lit.
  • Click on the buttons in the correct sequence to progress to the next level.
  • Enjoy playing Simon!
